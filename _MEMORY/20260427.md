### 1. Phase 2 — Topstep Simulator Refinement

Refined Topstep simulator in [`topstep_sim.py`](../pipeline/analysis/topstep_sim.py):
- `score = pass_rate - fail_mll_rate` (range -1.0 to +1.0, higher = better, maximizes pass while penalizing MLLs)
- Handle zero-trade days gracefully
- Reproducible windows across simulators

### 2. Comparison Test (`test_refined_sim.py`)

Ran [`test_refined_sim.py`](../pipeline/analysis/test_refined_sim.py) to compare old Topstep Pass Engine (v1) vs new Simulator (v2):

| Simulator | Score | Pass | Fail MLL | PnL |
|-----------|:----:|:----:|:--------:|:---:|
| Old (topstep_pass_engine.py) | +0.325 | 57.8% | 25.3% | $2,726 |
| New (topstep_sim.py) | +0.494 | 49.4% | 0.0% | $3,161 |

The old engine double-counted the same trades in multiple windows (look-ahead!), showing higher pass rates but at the cost of 25% fail-MLL. The new simulator is stricter and more realistic.
